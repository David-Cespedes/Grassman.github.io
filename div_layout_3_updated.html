Description of Less Commonly Known Books

Skulduggery Pleasant is a book where this girl "Stephanie" learns about how there has been a whole world of magic beneath her and she hadn't even known it. Now she has to find out who murdered her uncle while also trying to learn Elemental Magic so that she, Valkyrie Cain, can fight alongside Skulduggery Pleasant.
